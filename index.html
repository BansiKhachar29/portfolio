Master's Capstone

Ontario International Airport (ONT) —
Measuring Community Engagement ROI

2K Survey Respondents
0K+ Airport WiFi Records

Analytical Objective 3

Identify digital and geotargeting processes to track audience behavior after in-person or event-based engagement — directly addressing ONT's inability to connect community engagement activities to tangible business outcomes. While ONT's events generate thousands of face-to-face interactions annually, these touchpoints currently lack attribution data demonstrating conversion from awareness to action.

  • → Scored 43 ZIP codes using a weighted model — balancing post-event intent, competitor airport usage, and brand unawareness — to identify the highest-priority geofencing targets.
  • → Cross-validated ZIP code scores against 147K+ real airport WiFi login records to confirm which neighborhoods were already producing ONT passengers.
  • → Analyzed six media channels to find which ones actually convert — not just reach — using ONT's own survey data as the benchmark.
  • → Tested how many digital touchpoints are needed to increase airport use intent after the event — and built the post-event sequence around that threshold.
